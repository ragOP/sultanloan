About easyinstantloan.org

easyinstantloan.org is a referral service that helps connect Canadians with lenders and financial products. We provide information and access to instant personal loans of up to $40,000 through the 2026 Cup Loan Program.

Our mission is to help Canadians access financial assistance for holidays, bills, credit card debt, and other personal expenses. We work with trusted lending partners to offer quick and convenient loan options.
